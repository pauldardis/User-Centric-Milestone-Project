About

Cycle Tours was established in March 2017 to deliver a personal and bespoke cycling tours for cyclists of all abilities. We specialise in providing a personal and bespoke service as well as all-inclusive flexible cycling holidays and training camps for individuals and groups. We take the stress out of your trip by organising and providing everything, including ground arrangements, flights and accommodation. Whether you want to explore new destinations or you have a set of specific goals to improve your endurance, skills and fitness, we can tailor a unique experience together for you and a group, or you can just take one of our carefully designed all-inclusive packages.

At Cycle Tours we recognise that we need to provide a different experience for our customers. We understand it’s not just about the riding itself; it’s about the whole customer experience. From the moment you start researching, to the point at which you book your trip and arrive at your destination. We want to ensure our customers only have to focus on the cycling; the thing they most love! We have carefully chosen a unique set of partners to work with to ensure we deliver the best experience at the best price, with no compromise you don’t have to worry about a thing, just enjoy the cycling, relax and leave the rest to us.

Paul avid cyclist and traveller, Paul is the founder of Cycle Tours and his main focus is logistics and planning new tours. Paul's passion is ensuring that all guests experience a seamless experience from first contact through to completion of their tour. Favourite destination is Mallorca

Dave is the tour leader for the alps, he’s been cycling over 40 years with 10 years of competitive cycling, he is a fantastic mechanic and can rebuild a bike from scratch in a few hours. Favourite destination is Italy.

Gary just lives for the bike and spends the winter cycling in the Canary Islands. He has completed the Raid Pyrenees 5.

Misty is head of security and travels on all tours with Dave, she also acts as a counsellor for anyone having a bad day climbing wagging her tail as she waits to greet you at the top of each climb
